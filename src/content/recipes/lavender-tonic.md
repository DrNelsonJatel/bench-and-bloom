---
title: Lavender Tonic
description: The zero-proof gin and tonic. Tonic water, lavender syrup and lime, blooming pink. All the ritual, none of the alcohol.
kind: mocktail
servings: 1 drink
prepTime: PT2M
totalTime: PT2M
ingredients:
  - 1 oz Bench & Bloom lavender syrup
  - 0.5 oz fresh lime juice
  - Tonic water, to top
  - Ice and a lime wheel
steps:
  - Fill a glass with ice. Add the lavender syrup and the lime. It blooms pink.
  - Top with tonic water and stir gently.
  - Garnish with a lime wheel.

---

Tonic's quinine bitterness gives lavender a grown-up edge, so this drinks like a gin and tonic with
the gin left out. The lime turns it a soft pink and keeps it bright. For an even cleaner version, use
soda water instead of tonic.

Start from a bottle of [Bench & Bloom](/syrup) or a batch of
[lavender simple syrup](/recipes/how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup).
