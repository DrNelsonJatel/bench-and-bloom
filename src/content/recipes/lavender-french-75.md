---
title: Lavender French 75
description: The elevated one. Gin, lemon and lavender syrup lengthened with sparkling wine. The dinner-party pour.
kind: cocktail
spirit: Gin
servings: 1 drink
prepTime: PT5M
totalTime: PT5M
ingredients:
  - 1.5 oz gin
  - 0.5 oz lavender simple syrup
  - 0.5 oz fresh lemon juice
  - Sparkling wine, to top
  - Lemon twist, to garnish
steps:
  - Add the gin, lavender syrup and lemon juice to a shaker with ice.
  - Shake until cold and strain into a chilled flute or coupe.
  - Top with sparkling wine.
  - Express a lemon twist over the top and drop it in.

The French 75 is the lavender cocktail to pour when you want to feel a little fancy.
In wine country it almost makes itself: a local sparkling wine, a good gin and a
measure of lavender syrup.

Use a dry sparkling wine so the drink finishes crisp rather than sweet. Start from a
batch of [lavender simple syrup](/recipes/how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup) or a bottle
of [Bench & Bloom](/syrup).
