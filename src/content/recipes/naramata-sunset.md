---
title: Naramata Sunset
description: Our signature serve. Pour it blue, add the lemon, and watch it turn sunset pink, the colour of a Naramata evening over the lake.
kind: cocktail
spirit: Vodka
signature: true
servings: 1 drink
prepTime: PT3M
totalTime: PT3M
ingredients:
  - 2 oz vodka
  - 1 oz Bench & Bloom lavender syrup
  - Soda water, to top
  - Ice, a lemon wedge and a lavender sprig
steps:
  - Fill a tall glass with ice. Add the vodka and the lavender syrup.
  - Top with soda water and stir gently. It will be a deep blue.
  - Serve with a lemon wedge on the side. Squeeze and stir it in at the table.
  - Watch it bloom from blue to sunset pink. Garnish with a lavender sprig.

This is the drink Bench & Bloom was made for. It pours a clean blue, and the moment the lemon goes in
the whole glass turns pink, the same shift you see over Okanagan Lake at the end of a summer day.

Vodka keeps it clean and lets the lavender and the colour do the talking, but a botanical gin works
beautifully too. Start from a batch of
[lavender simple syrup](/recipes/how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup) or a bottle of
[Bench & Bloom](/syrup), which is already balanced for exactly this.
