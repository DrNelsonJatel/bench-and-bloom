---
title: Lavender Bee's Knees
description: Gin, lemon, honey and lavender, shaken bright. A floral twist on the Prohibition classic.
kind: cocktail
spirit: Gin
servings: 1 drink
prepTime: PT4M
totalTime: PT4M
ingredients:
  - 2 oz gin
  - 0.75 oz fresh lemon juice
  - 0.5 oz Bench & Bloom lavender syrup
  - 0.25 oz honey
  - A lemon twist
steps:
  - Add the gin, lemon, lavender syrup and honey to a shaker with ice.
  - Shake hard until cold and strain into a chilled coupe.
  - Express a lemon twist over the top and drop it in.
related:
  - lavender-gin-fizz
  - lavender-french-75
  - how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup
order: 24
---

Honey and lavender are old friends, and the Bee's Knees gives them a gin backbone. The lemon brightens
it and shifts the colour toward pink. Warm the honey slightly so it shakes in cleanly.

Start from a bottle of [Bench & Bloom](/syrup) or a batch of
[lavender simple syrup](/recipes/how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup).
