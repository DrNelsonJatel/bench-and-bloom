---
title: Lavender Gin Fizz
description: The classic. Gin, lemon, lavender syrup and soda, shaken bright and floral. The drink Bench & Bloom was built for.
kind: cocktail
spirit: Gin
servings: 1 drink
prepTime: PT5M
totalTime: PT5M
ingredients:
  - 2 oz gin
  - 0.75 oz lavender simple syrup
  - 0.75 oz fresh lemon juice
  - 1 egg white (optional, for foam)
  - Soda water, to top
  - Lavender sprig or lemon twist, to garnish
steps:
  - If using egg white, add the gin, lavender syrup, lemon and egg white to a shaker and dry shake (no ice) for ten seconds.
  - Add ice and shake hard until cold.
  - Strain into a chilled glass.
  - Top with soda water and garnish with a lavender sprig.

---

A botanical gin gives lavender something to lean on rather than fight. Look for a
juniper-forward or floral gin, and use plenty of fresh lemon. The citrus is what keeps
the lavender bright instead of perfumey.

Start with a batch of [lavender simple syrup](/recipes/how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup),
or pour a measure of [Bench & Bloom](/syrup), which is already balanced for exactly this.

Want it softer and longer? Make it a [Lavender Tom Collins](/recipes/lavender-lemonade)
style build with more soda, or skip the gin for a
[Lavender Lemonade](/recipes/lavender-lemonade).
