---
title: Lavender Lemonade
description: Mocktail or cocktail. Fresh lemon and lavender syrup over ice, with gin optional. The one everyone at the table can drink.
kind: mocktail
servings: 1 drink
prepTime: PT5M
totalTime: PT5M
ingredients:
  - 1 oz lavender simple syrup
  - 1 oz fresh lemon juice
  - 4 oz cold water or soda water
  - 1.5 oz gin (optional, to make it a cocktail)
  - Ice, lemon wheel and a lavender sprig
steps:
  - Fill a tall glass with ice.
  - Add the lavender syrup, lemon juice and the gin if you are using it.
  - Top with cold water for a still lemonade, or soda water for a sparkling one.
  - Stir, then garnish with a lemon wheel and a lavender sprig.
related:
  - how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup
  - lavender-latte
order: 30
---

This is the workhorse: bright, floral and endlessly adjustable. Leave the gin out and it
is a lavender lemonade the whole table can enjoy. Add a measure and it is a long, easy
cocktail for a hot Okanagan afternoon.

Adjust the syrup to taste, since lemons vary. Begin with a batch of
[lavender simple syrup](/recipes/how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup), or keep a bottle of
[Bench & Bloom](/syrup) in the fridge for exactly this.
