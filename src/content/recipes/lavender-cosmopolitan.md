---
title: Lavender Cosmopolitan
description: A floral cosmo. Vodka, cranberry, lime and lavender, shaken and served up.
kind: cocktail
spirit: Vodka
servings: 1 drink
prepTime: PT4M
totalTime: PT4M
ingredients:
  - 1.5 oz vodka
  - 0.75 oz cranberry juice
  - 0.5 oz fresh lime juice
  - 0.5 oz Bench & Bloom lavender syrup
  - A lime twist
steps:
  - Add the vodka, cranberry, lime and lavender syrup to a shaker with ice.
  - Shake until cold and strain into a chilled coupe.
  - Garnish with a lime twist.
related:
  - lavender-moscow-mule
  - naramata-sunset
  - how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup
order: 34
---

The cosmo is built for a floral lift, and lavender slots right in beside the cranberry and lime. The
citrus keeps it on the pink side of the colour shift, which suits the drink.

Start from a bottle of [Bench & Bloom](/syrup) or a batch of
[lavender simple syrup](/recipes/how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup).
