---
title: Iced Lavender Latte
description: No alcohol, all flavour. Espresso, milk and lavender syrup over ice. The cafe drink that made lavender mainstream, made at home.
kind: mocktail
servings: 1 drink
prepTime: PT5M
totalTime: PT5M
ingredients:
  - 2 shots espresso or 1/2 cup strong coffee, cooled
  - 0.5 to 1 oz lavender simple syrup, to taste
  - 1 cup milk or oat milk
  - Ice
steps:
  - Fill a tall glass with ice.
  - Add the lavender syrup and the milk, and stir.
  - Pour the cooled espresso over the top.
  - Stir once more before drinking.
related:
  - how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup
  - lavender-lemonade
order: 40
---

Lavender went mainstream in the coffee shop, not the bar, and an iced lavender latte is
the easiest way to use the syrup every day. Lavender and coffee are a natural pair, with
the floral note cutting through the roast.

Start light on the syrup and add to taste. Make a batch of
[lavender simple syrup](/recipes/how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup), or reach for a bottle
of [Bench & Bloom](/syrup).
