---
title: Lavender Ginger Fizz
description: Ginger ale, lime and lavender over ice. A bright, alcohol-free fizz with a little snap.
kind: mocktail
servings: 1 drink
prepTime: PT2M
totalTime: PT2M
ingredients:
  - 1 oz Bench & Bloom lavender syrup
  - 0.5 oz fresh lime juice
  - Ginger ale or ginger beer, to top
  - Ice and a lime wheel
steps:
  - Fill a glass with ice. Add the lavender syrup and the lime. It blooms pink.
  - Top with ginger ale for a softer fizz, or ginger beer for more snap.
  - Stir once and garnish with a lime wheel.
related:
  - lavender-moscow-mule
  - lavender-lemonade
  - how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup
order: 48
---

This is the alcohol-free cousin of the mule: ginger and lime with a measure of lavender to round it
out. Ginger ale keeps it gentle; ginger beer gives it a proper kick. The lime turns it pink as it goes
in.

Start from a bottle of [Bench & Bloom](/syrup) or a batch of
[lavender simple syrup](/recipes/how-to-make-lavender-simple-syrup).
